Introduction

Blossom is a plant care app that helps users identify over 30,000 plant species, set personalized care reminders, and diagnose plant diseases through photos. It offers features like a water calculator, gardening schedule, light meter (Android), and a personalized plant collection to organize your garden and wishlist. The app also provides tailored care tips and seasonal planting guidance for edible plants. Available on iOS and Android, Blossom makes plant care simple and accessible for all experience levels..

Problem Space

How might we build foundational knowledge and habits over time in order to help users prevent, rather than react to indoor plant death and disease?

Goals

  • Improve plant care habits with additional guidance and actionable tasks.
  • Find an engaging and meaningful way for users to complete their tasks.

    • Research Findings

    Articles

    “The average plant parent has killed 7 houseplants and 48% of them are worried about keeping their plants alive. ” (New York Botanical Garden)
    "Houseplant owners hold themselves responsible for failing to take care of their houseplants properly, but they will keep trying to buy new plants and take care of them." (2023)
    "While a significant number of American households own at least one houseplant, their understanding of plant care often doesn't extend much beyond basic watering and sunlight requirements. Many respondents lacked knowledge about more specific aspects of plant care, such as ideal soil types and fertilization needs​" (National Gardening Association (NGA))
    "Detailed botanical knowledge and advanced plant care techniques are mostly confined to dedicated enthusiasts and experts. This research suggests that most people possess only a superficial understanding of plant care, focusing primarily on the basic needs​" (University of Reading)

    Plant Care Books

  • Books like "The Houseplant Handbook" by David Squire and "How Not to Kill Your Houseplant" by Veronica Peerless provide insights into common knowledge gaps among casual plant owners. These resources are often sought after by those looking to improve their basic plant care skills.

    • Ratings & Reviews

  • 4+ Stars: Favoured journaling aspect, reminders
  • 1 Stars: Mixed reviews on scheduling

    • User Experiences

  • Experienced Plant Owner: Has owned a lot of plants over the years.
  • Casual Plant Owner: Owns some plants over the years.
  • Beginner Plant Owner: Just bought a plant.
