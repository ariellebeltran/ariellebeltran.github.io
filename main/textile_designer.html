    • Introduction

    GriplessGaunt is a product made for those with severe carpel tunnel syndrome. During the planning stages we talked about creating multiple attachments to fit a user's needs when they are in the bathroom. Such scenarios include using a razor, water pick, and toothbrush. To support these different types of attachments we concluded with creating a full brace with attachments so that the user is able to meet their needs in this type of environment. My role for this project was mainly constructing, sewing, and designing the hand brace.
