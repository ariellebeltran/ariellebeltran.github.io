    • Introduction

    This is a final project done in teams of 4, however my role and contribution was standalone to the rest of the project deliverables as I primarily worked on design mockups and a majority of slide deck design. Other roles for this project included copywriters and another graphic designer who mainly drew vector images. Since placeholder text was mostly used to display paragraphs, the copywriter's contributions are also not present in this work.


    HAO Electric LTD. is an electrical company based in Metro Vancouver, founded and led by Jimmy Zhen. Some of their services include LED lighting upgrades, electrical renovations, and EV charging stations installations for commercial, industrial, and residential.

    HAO Electric's goal is to obtain new clients, where it is noted that the the initial in-person meetings are crucial in securing clients. Currently, Jimmy conducts sit-down-meetings with prospective clients to answering any queries about his company and services. This approach is casual and is usually only effective for residential or lower-stakes clients.

    Objective

  • Give HAO Electric the tools that effectively showcase their portfolio and establish their ethos as a company.
  • Make meetings with the clients more efficient and engaging so that they retain exactly what they need from HAO Electric.
