    We chose to redesign the mobile app of Facebook so that it is more senior friendly and provides a better communication platform for seniors who use social media apps but are in need of a better interface that is suited for their abilities and specific wants.

    Objective

    Redesign an existing app designed for mainstream users to address a new target audience: Seniors that are 75 years or older.
